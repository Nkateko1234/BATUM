BATUM Celebrates 10 Years with Store Openings, Designer Collaborations, and Exciting Product Launches

BATUM, a proudly South African fashion brand, is marking a major milestone: 10 years of delivering confidence through clothing. In celebration, BATUM is launching three new flagship stores in Cape Town, Durban, and Pretoria this October — expanding its national presence and bringing bold, affordable fashion to even more customers. As part of this exciting chapter, BATUM is celebrating a record-breaking achievement of over 10 thousand units sold nationwide, solidifying its status as one of the country’s fastest-growing fashion retailers. To further mark the occasion, BATUM is launching two major collections: the vibrant Holiday 2025 line and a brand-new Activewear Series designed for comfort, movement, and style. Additionally, BATUM is collaborating with up-and-coming South African designers to release exclusive, limited-edition pieces that showcase a blend of tradition and trend. Customers are invited to join the grand opening celebrations, which will feature live entertainment, giveaways, launch-day discounts, and early access to the new collections. With a decade of growth behind it and a bold future ahead, BATUM continues to redefine fashion for the modern, confident South African.
